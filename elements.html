Thanks for visiting — here’s a quick intro!

With Four years of industry experience and a Master’s degree in Business Analytics from the University of Minnesota, I specialize in transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive strategic decision-making.

I bring hands-on expertise in data processing and analysis using tools like SQL, Python, R, Alteryx, and SAS. My strength lies in delivering intuitive, high-impact visualizations through platforms such as Tableau, Power BI, and Qlik Sense. Additionally, my proficiency in Qlik NPrinting enables me to design immersive web-based reporting solutions tailored for business users.

Beyond technical skills, I offer strong communication, analytical thinking, and a flexible, solution-oriented mindset. With a solid foundation in the finance sector, I combine domain knowledge with data fluency to craft intelligent, strategy-driven outcomes.

Fueled by curiosity and a commitment to continuous learning, I actively seek opportunities to expand my expertise and connect with like-minded professionals. Whether exploring emerging trends or refining established practices, I’m passionate about making data work smarter for people, products, and progress.

Outside of work, I’m a huge skating enthusiast—nothing clears my mind like gliding across the rink. And here’s a fun fact: I can stay awake for an entire day powered only by coffee!

If our interests align, I’d love to connect or collaborate. Let’s turn data into direction.
