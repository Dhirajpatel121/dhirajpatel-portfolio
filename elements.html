About Me!

I am Dhiraj Patel, a data enthusiast with a Bachelor of Engineering from Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, graduating with a CGPA of 9.28/10. With over four years of experience in the analytics domain, I've honed my skills and expertise to deliver impactful solutions that drive business growth and efficiency.

In my most recent role as a Senior Data Scientist at Think360.AI, I've led projects that have made significant contributions to client success. For instance, I spearheaded the development of predictive models for a large-scale Indian bank, resulting in substantial savings and enhanced decision-making capabilities. Additionally, I've collaborated with cross-functional teams to streamline processes and deliver top-quality solutions that meet and exceed client expectations.

Prior to this, I worked as a Data Scientist at Think360.AI, where I gained valuable experience in leveraging data and analytics to uncover insights and drive strategic decision-making. I've also had the opportunity to contribute to various projects, such as developing real-time fraud detection systems and automating data collection processes, which have had a positive impact on business operations.

Outside of my professional endeavors, I am deeply committed to continuous learning and growth. I actively engage in academic and co-curricular projects to expand my knowledge and skillset, and I'm always seeking out new challenges and opportunities to further develop my expertise in the analytics field.

Driven by a passion for data and a desire to make a meaningful impact, I am excited to continue my journey in the analytics domain and contribute to the success of organizations through innovative data-driven solutions.
